Naomi

Naomi (mother in law of Ruth)

Associations

  • Married to Elimelech (Ruth 1:1-2)
  • Visited Moab (Ruth 1:1)
  • Parent of Mahlon (Ruth 1:2)
  • Parent of Kilion (Ruth 1:2)
  • Enemy of Yahweh (Ruth 1:13)
  • Resident of Bethlehem (Ruth 1:19)
  • Name matches Mara (Ruth 1:20)
  • Enemy of Shaddai (Ruth 1:20-21)
  • Parent of Ruth (Ruth 1:2)
  • Blessed Boaz (Ruth 2:19)
  • Parent of Obed (Ruth 4:17)
  • Related to Elimelech's Redeemer (Ruth 3:12)
