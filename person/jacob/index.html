    Jacob

    Jacob (son of Isaac)

    Associations

    • Associated with sons of Hamor (Genesis 33:19)
    • Child of Rebekah (Genesis 25:24, Genesis 28:5), Isaac (Genesis 25:24, Genesis 35:29)
    • Cursed Rachel (Genesis 31:32)
    • Married to Leah (Genesis 29:23), Rachel (Genesis 29:28), Bilhah (Genesis 30:4), Zilpah (Genesis 30:9)
    • Name matches Israel (Genesis 32:28)
    • Parent of Reuben (Genesis 29:32, Genesis 35:23), Simeon (Genesis 29:33, Genesis 34:25, Genesis 35:23), Levi (Genesis 29:34, Genesis 34:25, Genesis 35:23), Judah (Genesis 29:35, Genesis 35:23), Dan (Genesis 30:5, Genesis 35:25), Naphtali (Genesis 30:7, Genesis 35:25), Gad (Genesis 30:10, Genesis 35:26), Asher (Genesis 30:12, Genesis 35:26), Issachar (Genesis 30:18, Genesis 35:23), Zebulun (Genesis 30:20, Genesis 35:23), Dinah (Genesis 30:21), Joseph (Genesis 30:24, Genesis 35:24), Dinah (Genesis 34:1), Benjamin (Genesis 35:18, Genesis 35:24)
    • Resident of Succoth (Genesis 33:17), Shechem (Genesis 33:18), Eder (Genesis 35:21)
    • Visited Paddan Aram (Genesis 28:5), Isaac (Genesis 35:27), Mamre (Genesis 35:27)
