    Jacob

    Jacob (son of Isaac)

    Associations

    • Associated with sons of Hamor (Genesis 33:19), sword (Genesis 48:22), bow (Genesis 48:22), Machpelah (Genesis 49:29)
    • Attacked Amorites (Genesis 48:22)
    • Blessed Pharaoh (Genesis 47:7), Joseph (Genesis 48:15), Ephraim (Genesis 48:16), Manasseh (Genesis 48:16)
    • Buried in Machpelah (Genesis 50:13)
    • Child of Rebekah (Genesis 25:24, Genesis 28:5), Isaac (Genesis 25:24, Genesis 35:29), Jacob (Genesis 46:8)
    • Cursed Rachel (Genesis 31:32), Reuben (Genesis 49:4)
    • Died in Goshen (Genesis 49:33)
    • Married to Leah (Genesis 29:23, Genesis 46:15), Rachel (Genesis 29:28, Genesis 46:19), Bilhah (Genesis 30:4, Genesis 46:25), Zilpah (Genesis 30:9, Genesis 46:18)
    • Name matches Israel (name matches Jacob) (Genesis 32:28)
    • Parent of Reuben (Genesis 29:32, Genesis 35:23, Genesis 46:9, Genesis 48:5), Simeon (Genesis 29:33, Genesis 34:25, Genesis 35:23, Genesis 46:10, Genesis 48:5), Levi (Genesis 29:34, Genesis 34:25, Genesis 35:23, Genesis 46:11), Judah (Genesis 29:35, Genesis 35:23, Genesis 46:12), Dan (Genesis 30:5, Genesis 35:25, Genesis 46:23), Naphtali (Genesis 30:7, Genesis 35:25, Genesis 46:24), Gad (Genesis 30:10, Genesis 35:26, Genesis 46:16), Asher (Genesis 30:12, Genesis 35:26, Genesis 46:17), Issachar (Genesis 30:18, Genesis 35:23, Genesis 46:13), Zebulun (Genesis 30:20, Genesis 35:23, Genesis 46:14), Dinah (Genesis 30:21, Genesis 34:1, Genesis 46:15), Joseph (Genesis 30:24, Genesis 35:24, Genesis 46:19), Benjamin (Genesis 35:18, Genesis 35:24, Genesis 46:19), Beriah (Genesis 46:17), Ephraim (Genesis 48:5), Manasseh (Genesis 48:5)
    • Resident of Succoth (Genesis 33:17), Shechem (Genesis 33:18), Eder (Genesis 35:21), Paddan Aram (Genesis 46:15), Goshen (Genesis 46:28), Rameses (Genesis 47:11)
    • Visited Paddan Aram (Genesis 28:5), Isaac (Genesis 35:27), Mamre (Genesis 35:27), Beer Sheba (Genesis 46:1)
