    Esau

    Esau (son of Isaac)

    Associations

    • Child of Rebekah (Genesis 25:24, Genesis 28:5), Isaac (Genesis 25:24, Genesis 35:29)
    • Married to Judith (Genesis 26:34), Basemath (Genesis 26:34, Genesis 36:3), Mahalath (Genesis 28:9), Adah (Genesis 36:2), Oholibamah (Genesis 36:2)
    • Name matches Edomite (descendants of Esau) (Genesis 36:1, Genesis 36:9), Edom (place where Esau lived) (Genesis 36:1, Genesis 36:9)
    • Origin of Edomite (Genesis 36:9)
    • Parent of Eliphaz (Genesis 36:10, Genesis 36:4), Reuel (Genesis 36:10, Genesis 36:5), Jeush (Genesis 36:5), Jalam (Genesis 36:5), Korah (Genesis 36:5)
    • Resident of Seir (Genesis 36:8)
