    Isaac

    Isaac (son of Abraham)

    Associations

    • Associated with Abimelech (Genesis 26:1)
    • Blessed Jacob (Genesis 27:27, Genesis 28:1), Esau (Genesis 27:39)
    • Buried in Mamre (Genesis 35:29)
    • Child of Sarah (Genesis 21:2), Abraham (Genesis 21:2, Genesis 25:19)
    • Died in Mamre (Genesis 35:29)
    • Married to Rebekah (Genesis 24:67, Genesis 25:20)
    • Parent of Jacob (Genesis 25:24, Genesis 35:29), Esau (Genesis 25:24, Genesis 35:29)
    • Related to Bethuel (Genesis 28:1)
    • Resident of Beer Lahai Roi (Genesis 25:11), Gerar (Genesis 26:1)
    • Sent Jacob (Genesis 28:1)
