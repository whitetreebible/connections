    Elimelech (husband of Naomi)

    Associations

    • Mentioned with plague (Ruth 1:1)
    • Married to Naomi (Ruth 1:1-2)
    • Resident of Bethlehem (Ruth 1:1-2)
    • Visited Moab (Ruth 1:1)
    • Parent of Mahlon (Ruth 1:2)
    • Parent of Kilion (Ruth 1:2)
    • Died in Moab (Ruth 1:3)
    • Related to Boaz (Ruth 2:1)
    • Related to Elimelech's Redeemer (Ruth 3:12)
